Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your data never leaves your device

DayTracker is a personal habit and activity tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

DayTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Local Storage

All data you create in the app — including habits, tracking logs, streaks, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses your device's local notification system to schedule alerts. No data is sent to any server to deliver these notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

DayTracker does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at your@email.com.
